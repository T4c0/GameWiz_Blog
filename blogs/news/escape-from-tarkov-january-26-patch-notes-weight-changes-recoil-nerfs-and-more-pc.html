Escape From Tarkov January 26 Patch Notes - Weight, Recoil Nerfs and More

January 26, 2023
| GameWiz

New new

Escape from Tarkov has gained a lot of popularity and a devoted player base thanks to its distinctive blend of tactical and survival aspects.

Resetting the game's servers and player progress is known as "wiping," and it's frequently done to start over with new features and content.

Escape From Tarkov January 26 Patch Notes - Weight Changes, Recoil Nerfs and More

The most recent wipe has caused a lot of excitement in the community because it has allowed people to start over and enjoy the game in a different way. Additionally, because they are not concerned with being at a disadvantage when compared to more seasoned players, it has encouraged new players to attempt the game.

The latest patch's specifics were revealed via Twitter, and they mostly address the users' top complaints about the game since the significant wipe.

Here's all the changes to EFT today.

  • * Reduced the recoil angle for all weapons.
  • * Reduced the stamina penalty when hit by any type of ammo.
  • * Increase the weight limit while overweight.
  • * Reduced stamina consumption in various cases, decreased energy and water consumption in raids.
  • * Greatly reduced penalties from armored rigs and body armor.
